common tools


1. soldering iron

this is one of important tool when it come to electronics world , it is there to soder and unsoder things

uses of soldering iron

  • soldering thing to different boards
  • unsoldering different components from different boards


2. screwdrivers

these are tools that we use to screw or unscrew things like board in this case.

uses of screwdrivers

  • unscrewing screws
  • screwing


3. hot air machine

It is used for shrinkage of security straps on cylinder valves. It is a hot air blowing system

uses of hot air machine

  • Salvaging electronic components from old PCBs
  • Shrinking heat-shrink to secure wire connections.
  • removing parts from an old circuit board
  • Reflowing solder paste applied with a stencil between SMD components and a PCB.
  • Heating up and bending certain plastics (with the correct settings).
