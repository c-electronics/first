types of capacitors


1. ceramic capacitor

a ceramic capacitor is considered to be one of the most commonly used capacitors. The material used in this capacitor type is dielectric

applications of ceramic capacitors

  • Ceramic capacitors are used in printed circuit boards that are used in high-density applications
  • Their non-polarity makes them suitable for general usage
  • They find applications in DC motors as they are used for reducing the RF noise.
  • Ceramic capacitors are used in transmitter stations where resonant circuits are used.


2. dielectric capacitor

A typical capacitor comprises two conductive plates and a non-conductive dielectric material.

uses of dielectric capacitors

  • storage of energy in the electric field between the plates
  • filtering out noise from signals as part of a resonant circuit
  • supplying a burst of power to another component.
