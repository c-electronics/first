types of transistors


1. phototransistor

a Phototransistor is an electronic switching and current amplification component which relies on exposure to light to operate

applications of phototransistor

  • Position sensing
  • infrared receiver
  • CD players
  • lasers.


2.field effect transistor (FETs)

A typical capacitor comprises two conductive plates and a non-conductive dielectric material.

uses of FETs

  • voltage-variable resistors (WRs) in operational amplifiers (op-amps) and tone control
  • or mixer operation on FM and TV receivers and in logic circuits.
  • igital switching circuits though their operating speed is lower.


2.bipolar junction transistors (BJTs)

A typical capacitor comprises two conductive plates and a non-conductive dielectric material.

uses of BJTs

  • as an amplifier or as aswitch
  • as a filter
  • as a rectifier or as an oscillator
